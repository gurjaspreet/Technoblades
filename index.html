In many communities worldwide, small-scale tomato farmers play a vital role in ensuring food security. However, these farmers often face challenges accessing fair markets due to limited resources and bargaining power, leaving them vulnerable to exploitation by middlemen. The solution lies in forming cooperatives, where small-scale tomato farmers can unite to access markets collectively. This approach not only strengthens their bargaining position but also enables the sharing of essential resources and knowledge. Together, through cooperation, these farmers can pave the way for a sustainable and prosperous future for both themselves and their communities.
